About Monster Survivors

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Collect experience to level up, choose powerful abilities, and survive as long as possible in this challenging adventure.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby monsters as you move. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones. Survive as long as possible to achieve the highest score!

Multiple Characters

Choose from different heroes, each with unique starting abilities and playstyles.

Diverse Abilities

Unlock and upgrade various weapons and passive abilities to create powerful combinations.

Epic Boss Fights

Face challenging boss monsters that will test your skills and strategy.

Pro Tips & Strategies

  • •

    Keep moving: Standing still is deadly. Always stay on the move to avoid being surrounded.

  • •

    Prioritize upgrades: Focus on a few powerful abilities rather than spreading your upgrades too thin.

  • •

    Collect all gems: Experience gems disappear after a while, so make sure to collect them quickly.

  • •

    Watch for item chests: They contain powerful equipment that can turn the tide of battle.
